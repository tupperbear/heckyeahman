The Details

You really can have it all. Land, ocean, privacy and a modern home all within minutes of the UNESCO world heritage town of Lunenburg. This energy efficient, 13 year old, 2230 sq/ft home sits on 1.7 acres surrounded by over 400 feet of protected ocean frontage. Perfect for paddling, boating or simply enjoying the stunning sunsets.

The home has been meticulously cared for; upgrades include renovated bathrooms, full kitchen renovation, new drilled well, pump, pressure tank, water softener, new engineered hardwood throughout the bottom floor, new 300 sq/ft deck, new tile throughout house. The property also enjoys two new out buildings: a 28x12 workshop with an insulated concrete slab foundation, fully insulated structure, new electrical service, wiring and new siding, as well as solid, hand built, 12x8 shed for additional storage.

This is truly a property that needs to be experienced. Contact us anytime to book a visit or request any additional information.
